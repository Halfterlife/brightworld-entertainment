《翻雪》隐私政策

最新更新日期： 2026年2月

欢迎您使用“翻雪”App。本应用由陽間世界娛樂有限公司开发并运营。我们非常重视您的隐私，并承诺保护您的个人信息。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们在您使用翻雪时如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

  • 账号信息： 当您注册翻雪账号时，我们会收集您提供的用户名和密码，用于创建和管理您的专属账号。
  • 阅读与使用数据： 为了提供书架同步、阅读历史记录和签到服务，我们会记录您的书架藏书、阅读进度、每日阅读时长以及签到状态。
  • 交易记录： 当您在应用内购买书币或订阅VIP时，我们会记录您的订单信息（如Apple提供的交易凭证），以便为您发放对应的应用内资产。我们不会收集您的信用卡号或Apple ID密码，支付过程完全由苹果公司安全处理。
  • 反馈信息： 当您使用“意见反馈”功能时，我们会收集您填写的反馈内容及联系方式，仅用于解决您遇到的问题。

2. 信息的存储与保护

我们采用业界标准的安全技术措施来保护您的数据。您的账号和阅读数据存储在高度安全的服务器上，且密码经过加密处理。尽管如此，请您妥善保管您的账号密码，避免信息泄露。

3. 信息的共享与披露

我们承诺不会将您的个人信息出售、出租或交易给任何第三方。我们仅在以下情况可能会披露信息：

  • 获得您的明确同意；
  • 响应法律、法规或司法程序的强制性要求。

4. 账号注销与数据删除

您有权随时注销您的翻雪账号。您可以在App内“我的账户”或“个人中心”页面找到“注销账号”选项。账号注销后，您的个人信息、阅读记录及应用内资产（包括书币、书券、VIP权益）将被永久删除且无法恢复。

5. 联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

邮箱：support@brightworldentertainment.com

Fanxue Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to the "Fanxue" App. This application is developed and operated by Brightworld Entertainment Limited. We highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use Fanxue.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register an account, we collect your username and password to create and manage your profile.
  • Reading and Usage Data: To provide features like bookshelf synchronization, reading history, and daily check-ins, we record your saved books, reading progress, daily reading time, and check-in status.
  • Transaction Records: When you purchase Book Coins or subscribe to VIP within the app, we record your order information (such as transaction receipts provided by Apple) to grant you the corresponding in-app assets. We do not collect your credit card number or Apple ID password; the payment process is securely handled entirely by Apple Inc.
  • Feedback Information: When you use the "Feedback" feature, we collect the content and contact information you provide, solely for the purpose of resolving your issues.

2. Data Storage and Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your account and reading data are stored on highly secure servers, and passwords are encrypted. However, please keep your account password safe to prevent information leakage.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We promise not to sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We may only disclose information in the following situations:

  • With your explicit consent;
  • In response to mandatory requirements by laws, regulations, or judicial proceedings.

4. Account Deletion and Data Removal

You have the right to delete your Fanxue account at any time. You can find the "Delete Account" option in the "My Account" or "Profile" page within the App. Upon account deletion, your personal information, reading history, and in-app assets (including Book Coins, Book Coupons, and VIP subscriptions) will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@brightworldentertainment.com
